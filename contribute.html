Your financial support advances scientific research and the training of next-generation Forager Archaeology Lab scholars. We welcome donations of any amount. $20 pays for a day of field research for one of our scholars. $200 gets us a radiocarbon date. $20,000 funds a season of excavation or an academic quarter of graduate student research and course fees. If you are interested in making a contribution, please contact Dr. Randy Haas.
